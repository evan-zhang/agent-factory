## 原文内容

Intract Pharma and Tharimmune (THAR) announced positive preclinical results for TH023 (oral infliximab) in March 2025.

Key findings:
- SoteriaTM platform: proprietary formulation of natural amino acids for enzymatic protection
- Successful in-vivo delivery of infliximab into colonic tissue and systemic circulation
- Mechanism: combination of passive + neonatal FcRn mediated active transport
- Superior to SNAC permeation enhancer for antibody delivery
- Plans: first-in-human trial within next 12 months (from March 2025)
- Tharimmune licensed global rights (outside South Korea) from Intract

Infliximab global sales (including biosimilars): ~$6.3B in 2022
Experts suggest market could rise to $9B within 10 years
