Ulcerative Colitis Drug Market size estimates:
- 2024: USD 7.72 Bn (Maximize Market Research)
- 2026: USD 10.16 Bn (Coherent Market Insights)
- 2032: USD 12.03 Bn projected (CAGR 5.7%)
- 2031: USD 15.18-15.99 Bn projected

By drug class:
- Anti-TNF biologics led with 37.88% revenue share in 2025
- JAK inhibitors forecast to expand at 13.68% CAGR through 2031
- Biologics (Anti-TNF, Integrin inhibitors, IL inhibitors) held 37.75% market share in 2025

Key trends:
- Growth primarily driven by increasing use of non-TNF biologics (IL inhibitors, integrin inhibitors)
- JAK inhibitors expanding rapidly
- Clarivate expects steady growth over next decade
- Substantial proportion of patients do not respond, lose response, or develop intolerance to current products
