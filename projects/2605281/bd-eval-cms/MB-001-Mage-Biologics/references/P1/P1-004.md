## 原文内容

Title: A Study of MB-001 in Moderately to Severely Active Ulcerative Colitis

Description: The goal of this clinical trial is to learn if MB-001, an oral biologic, is able to treat patients with ulcerative colitis.

Status: Phase 1/Phase 2
Sponsor: Mage Biologics Inc.
Registration: NCT07374471

Note: This is the I/II 期 trial for UC patients (following Phase 1 healthy volunteer study NCT06363383).
