## 原文内容

Mage Biologics has launched a significant clinical trial initiative in Newark, DE, USA. On June 12, 2024, the company announced it had administered the first dose of MB-001 to a study participant. MB-001 is a cutting-edge orally administered antibody specifically designed to treat inflammatory bowel disease (IBD).

The clinical trial, registered under NCT06363383, is a randomized, double-blind Phase 1 study focusing on the safety and pharmacokinetics of MB-001. The trial will involve single and multiple ascending doses administered to adult healthy volunteers. Mage Biologics aims to complete this study by mid-2025.

Key collaboration details:
- Alimentiv: GI-specialized CRO
- Chorus (Eli Lilly): autonomous R&D unit supporting innovative clinical plan
- Tillotts Pharma: part of Japanese Zeria Group, ~400 employees, markets IBD and CDI products in ~65 countries
- TVM Capital Life Science: international VC firm, ~$900M under management, unique PFC (Project-Focused Company) approach with Eli Lilly strategic relationship
